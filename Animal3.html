Penguin


Penguins are a group of aquatic flightless birds.
They live almost exclusively in the Southern Hemisphere:
only one species, the Galápagos penguin, is found north
of the Equator. Highly adapted for life in the water, penguins
have countershaded dark and white plumage and flippers for swimming.


Polar bear

The polar bear is a hypercarnivorous bear whose native
range lies largely within the Arctic Circle, encompassing
the Arctic Ocean, its surrounding seas and surrounding land
masses. It is the largest extant bear species, as well as the
largest extant land carnivore.

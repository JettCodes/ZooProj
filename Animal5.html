Rainforest Habitat

A beam of sunlight makes its way through the leaves and lights up the brightly
colored wings of a macaw. An enormous butterfly flits from plant to plant. Monkeys
shout and chatter as they swing from tree to tree looking for breakfast. The rainforest
is waking up.

Mountain Gorilla

The mountain gorilla is one of the two subspecies of the eastern gorilla.
It is listed as endangered by the IUCN as of 2018

Tapir

Tapirs are large, herbivorous mammals belonging to the family Tapiridae.
They are similar in shape to a pig, with a short, prehensile nose trunk.
Tapirs inhabit jungle and forest regions of South and Central America,
with one species inhabiting Southeast Asia.
